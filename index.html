Privacy Policy for Fruity Splash

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Fruity Splash is a mobile puzzle game published by Hieplex. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses, how it is used, and the choices available to players.

Information We Collect

Fruity Splash may collect or store the following information:

  • Game progress, including unlocked levels, scores, stars, coins, boosters, energy, and settings.
  • Optional Google sign-in information, such as your Google account identifier, when you choose to sign in.
  • Cloud economy data, such as coins, boosters, and energy, when cloud sync is available and you are signed in.
  • Purchase information from Google Play Billing, such as product ID and transaction information for coin packs.
  • Basic technical information needed by app stores, Google Play services, Supabase, and device systems to operate the app.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Save and restore your game progress.
  • Sync your coins, boosters, and energy across sessions when you sign in.
  • Process and deliver in-app purchases, including gold coin packs.
  • Maintain gameplay features, settings, and account-related functionality.
  • Improve reliability, fix issues, and protect against duplicate purchase grants.

In-App Purchases

Fruity Splash offers optional in-app purchases through Google Play Billing. Payment information is processed by Google Play. Fruity Splash does not receive or store your full payment card details.

Third-Party Services

Fruity Splash may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play services for app distribution, sign-in support, and in-app purchases.
  • Google Play Billing for purchasing digital coin packs.
  • Supabase for optional account and cloud economy storage.

These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Local Storage

Some game progress and settings are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, local progress may be removed unless cloud sync or another backup method is available.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with service providers when needed to run the app, process purchases, provide sign-in, sync game progress, comply with legal requirements, or protect the app and players.

Children's Privacy

Fruity Splash is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children where parental consent is required by law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

Your Choices

  • You can play without signing in where the app allows local progress.
  • You can avoid optional in-app purchases.
  • You can request deletion of account-related cloud data by contacting us.

Account and Data Deletion

You can request deletion of account-related Fruity Splash data by following the instructions on our Account and Data Deletion page.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect game and account data. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
