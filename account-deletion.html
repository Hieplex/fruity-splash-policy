Account and Data Deletion for Fruity Splash

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This page explains how Fruity Splash players can request deletion of their account-related data. Fruity Splash is published by Hieplex.

How to Request Account and Data Deletion

  1. Email dauma12333@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Fruity Splash account deletion request.
  3. Include the Google account email address you used to sign in to Fruity Splash.
  4. We may ask for limited information to confirm that the request is from the account owner.

Data Deleted

When your deletion request is verified, we will delete account-related cloud data associated with Fruity Splash, including:

  • Cloud game economy data, such as coins, boosters, and energy.
  • Cloud progress or account records connected to your Fruity Splash sign-in.
  • Stored authentication/session records controlled by Fruity Splash, where applicable.

Data That May Be Kept

Some data may be retained when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or platform reasons, including:

  • Google Play purchase records that are maintained by Google Play.
  • Minimal transaction records needed to prevent duplicate purchase grants or abuse.
  • Records we are required to keep for legal, tax, accounting, dispute, or security purposes.

Retention Period

Verified deletion requests are normally processed within 30 days. Data that must be retained for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or transaction-record purposes may be kept for as long as required for those purposes.

Local Device Data

Fruity Splash may store gameplay progress locally on your device. You can delete local app data by clearing the app data in your device settings or uninstalling the app. Deleting local device data does not automatically delete cloud data, so please email us if you also want account-related cloud data deleted.

Partial Data Deletion

You may also email us to request deletion of some account-related cloud data without deleting all account data. Please describe the data you want deleted in your request.

Contact

For account and data deletion requests, contact dauma12333@gmail.com.
